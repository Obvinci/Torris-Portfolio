Overview

SynoPay is one of the most important project of Thinkship. SynoPay is a payment solutions company that provides technology, equipment and support to connect American merchants to Chinese customers. It offers the safest and easiest solution to connect American merchants to China's most popular mobile payment platforms — WeChat Pay, AliPay and China UnionPay. Our team has extensive experience in the American and Chinese financial technology markets, which makes SynoPay especially qualified to provide solutions for cross-border payments. Thinkship built a merchant onboarding management system for a payment solutions company that links US merchants with Chinese consumers. Features included a merchant tracking system and a secure document portal. In this project, I collaborated with the marketing team, sales team and technique development team to optimize plan and solutions for brand expansion in North America.

  • GOALS

    Develop a platform that would track the merchant onboarding process. Create a secure document portal, which could be used to upload, transmit, and store confidential information. Finally, a payout platform is required for calculating commissions based on complex transactions.
